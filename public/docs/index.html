Getting Started

Welcome

Tanzania Bureau of Standards (TBS) was established under the Ministry of Industry and Trade by an Act of Parliament, the Standards Act No.3 of 1975 as the National Standards Institute and became operational in April 1976. It was subsequently renamed Tanzania Bureau of Standards through an amendment to the Act by Act No.1 of 1977. The Standards Act No. 3 was later repealed and replaced by the Standards Act No. 2 of 2009, which gave the Bureau more powers in carrying out its mandate

The online Application system will enable TBS customers to submit applications online for the following services;

  • Destination Inspection
  • Conditional Release
  • PVoC
This is the user manual which will guide user on using the application.

Company Registration

If the company doesn’t have an account in the system, they should register by clicking the “Register Here” Button

The company registers by filling and submitting the required information and documents.

Registration Confirmation

Company must confirm registration process by entering the code sent through the company mobile number provided during registration, if the SMS has not been delivered, user can resend the code by clicking the Resend SMS button.

Company Director/CEO Details Registration

Immediately after Confirming the registration, User must register the company Director/CEO details by clicking Register Now button (this is a one-time procedure)

Impoter Registration

For the first time registration, Company must register the importers before starting the application process by clicking Register Now button.

The company registers its importers by filling and submitting the required information and documents.

To add other importer, Company navigates as indicated in the diagram below, and registers importer.

The company registers its importers by filling and submitting the required information and documents.

There are two types of importer who can be registered into the system

  • Individual Impoter

    • User can register Individual importers by filling and submitting the required information and documents.

  • Company Importer

    • User can register Company importers by filling and submitting the required information and documents.

    *ID Card – is the photocopy of the importers Identification Card whose information have been filled on the form.
    *Declaration letter- is the letter prepared by the importer to acknowledge and agree that the Clearing and Forward Agent company will conduct all clearing procedures on behalf of the importer.

Company Login

If the company has an account in the system, they can login by entering the company email address and password submitted during the registration.

New PVOC Application

Company makes new application by clicking the New Application button found on the dashboard

User Selects the registered importer and provides the B/L number and clicks Search button.

If the B/L number is found (Manifest) and has not already been used, the details of the B/L number will appear and user can continue with the application by clicking “Continue with application” button, otherwise Click Continue with Application button.

After filling the application information and upload required documents, user clicks next button to continue with the application

After filling the COC information and upload required documents, user clicks next button to continue with the application.

User acknowledges the terms by ticking the “I agree” checkbox and clicks the submit button to send the application to TBS for processing.
